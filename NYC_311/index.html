Parking is top complaint in NYC

A look into complaints in 311 data

By Christine

Looking through 311 data one can find all sorts of complaints. From rodents, to noise, to homelessness, to water issues. However, the number one complaint is illegal parking. In the month of June 2024 alone there were over 46,000 complaints for illegally parked vehicles.

Complaints consist of issues such as blocked hydrants, blocked sidewalks, blocked crosswalks, blocked bike lanes, and general parking sign violations.

Most parking complaints occur on Sundays, but there is only a small difference in the number of complaints reported during the week. As seen in the chart, complaints were made most frequently on Sundays and least frequently on Thursdays.

While parking complaints were most frequent, noise came in second. New Yorkers complained about residential noise around 31,000 times and street/sidewalk noise 25,000 times. If the noise complaints are combined noise actually surpasses parking as the top complaint in NYC.
