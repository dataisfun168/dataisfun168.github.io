"Mickey" is not a popular baby name



In NY state the name "Mickey" isn't very popular. For boys named "Mickey" popularity peaked in the 1950's. This corresponded to the airing of The Mickey Mouse Club tv show. We don't know if the tv show caused more individuals to name their sons Mickey, but shortly after there was an increase in the popularity of the name. In the late 1920s-1960's there were a few girls named Mickey, but for girls the name is even less popular.
